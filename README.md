YOGHURT SALES ANALYSIS 
PROJECT OVERVIEW
The project focuses on the design and implementation of an interactive Power BI dashboard, supported by advanced analytics, to evaluate yoghurt sales performance for the 2022 fiscal year.

OBJECTIVE 
The primary objective is to generate actionable business insights and deliver interactive visualizations that enhance decision‑making capabilities. By providing sales managers with accurate, data‑driven intelligence, the project aims to strengthen strategic planning and operational efficiency.

EXPECTED OUTCOMES

1.Analyze channels,regional and SKU sales perfomance.
2.Evaluate the effectiveness of promotional activities and analyze their impact on overall sales perfomance.
3.Improved ability for sales managers to identify opportunities, address challenges, and optimize sales strategies

KEY INSIGHTS
1.PL-South leads has the highest Revenue generated from sales at 33.37% with most revenue generated via Retail channel at 33.81%.
2.PL-North has the highest number of units sold with Ecommerce channel driving most sales at 33.4%.
3.YO-029 is the best performing SKU.
4.Promotional activities have low impact contributing to 26.64% sales in the market,indicating strong brand loyalty.
5.Peak sales observed in June and July with minimu sales in February and November.

TOOLS USED
Excel-Data cleaning &Preprocessing
PowerBI-Further data cleaning&transformation,dashboard creation&visualization

DATA SOURCE
The small data used was obtained from Kaggle(FMCG Daily Sales Data(2022-2024) dataset using VBA code and macro.
